ABOUT US

COMPANY PROFILE

Water is an essential part of life. Nowadays we are finding ourselves fighting over because of the water quality. Water become a serious topic around us as because we need to sustain our growing population. The truth is, our cities (and everything in it) depend on water. We all drink it and use it every day. In fact, according to the new report by UNICEF and the World Health Organization billions of people around the world are continuing to suffer from poor access to drinking water.In this scenario our WaterMan application will be a hope for people.

Our WaterMan application is a drinking water delivery app which helps hundreds of people for instantly order water cans with simple steps. We supply water cans, water bottles, large containers etc. Users can also have the choice to select their preferences and order a customized water bottles.

MISSION

Our mission is to create a world where no one suffer from lack of pure drinking water. We will try to establish our app world wide.
